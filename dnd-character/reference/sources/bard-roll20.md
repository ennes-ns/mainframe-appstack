# official Bard Class Rules (Roll20)

## Class Overview: Bard
*Source: Free Basic Rules (2014)*

As a bard, you gain the following class features.

### Hit Points
*   **Hit Dice:** 1d8 per bard level
*   **Hit Points at 1st Level:** 8 + your Constitution modifier
*   **Hit Points at Higher Levels:** 1d8 (or 5) + your Constitution modifier per bard level after 1st

### Starting Proficiencies
*   **Armor:** Light armor
*   **Weapons:** Simple weapons, hand crossbows, longswords, rapiers, shortswords
*   **Tools:** Three musical instruments of your choice
*   **Saving Throws:** Dexterity, Charisma
*   **Skills:** Choose any three.

### Starting Equipment
*   (a) a rapier, (b) a longsword, or (c) any simple weapon
*   (a) a diplomat's pack or (b) an entertainer's pack
*   (a) a lute or (b) any other musical instrument
*   Leather Armor, and a dagger

## Class Features

### Bardic Inspiration
You can inspire others through stirring words or music. As a bonus action, choose one creature within 60 feet. That creature gains one Bardic Inspiration die (**d6**).
*   Within 10 minutes, the creature can roll the die and add it to one ability check, attack roll, or saving throw.
*   Die size increases: **d8** (5th), **d10** (10th), **d12** (15th).

### Spellcasting
*   **Cantrips:** You know 2 at Level 1.
*   **Spell Slots:** Regain all on a long rest.
*   **Spellcasting Ability:** Charisma.
    *   **Spell save DC:** 8 + Proficiency + Charisma
    *   **Spell attack modifier:** Proficiency + Charisma
*   **Ritual Casting:** You can cast bard spells as rituals if they have the tag.
*   **Focus:** You can use a musical instrument as a focus.

### Jack of All Trades (2nd Level)
Add half your proficiency bonus (rounded down) to any ability check that doesn't already include it.

### Song of Rest (2nd Level)
Regain extra **1d6** HP during a short rest (increases to **d8**, **d10**, **d12** at higher levels).

### Bard College (3rd Level)
Choose a college (e.g., **College of Lore**). Grants features at 3rd, 6th, and 14th level.

### Expertise (3rd & 10th Level)
Double your proficiency bonus for two chosen skills.

### Ability Score Improvement (4th, 8th, 12th, 16th, 19th Level)
Increase one score by 2, or two scores by 1 (max 20).

### Font of Inspiration (5th Level)
Regain Bardic Inspiration on short OR long rests.

### Countercharm (6th Level)
Use an action to give allies advantage on saves against Frightened or Charmed effects.

### Magical Secrets (10th, 14th, 18th Level)
Learn two spells from any class. They count as bard spells.

### Superior Inspiration (20th Level)
Regain one use of Bardic Inspiration if you have none when rolling initiative.

## College of Lore
*Bards of the College of Lore know something about most things, collecting bits of knowledge from sources as diverse as scholarly tomes and peasant tales.*

1.  **Bonus Proficiencies (3rd Level):** Gain proficiency with three skills of your choice.
2.  **Cutting Words (3rd Level):** Use a reaction and an Inspiration die to subtract from a creature's attack, ability check, or damage roll.
3.  **Additional Magical Secrets (6th Level):** Learn two spells from any class (don't count against your spells known).
