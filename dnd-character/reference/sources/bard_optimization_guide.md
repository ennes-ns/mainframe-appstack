# Bard Optimization Guide (Lazarus Build)

*Source: Derived from RPGBOT Bard Class Guide*

## Class Role: The Jack of All Trades
Bards are incredibly versatile. Lazarus, as a **Face** and **Support/Controller**, should focus on:
- **Charisma:** Your primary stat for spells and social skills.
- **Expertise:** You already have this in *Insight* and *Performance*. Great choice for a "Face" character.

## Recommended Spells for Lazarus
Given your *College of the Chanter* (Virtuosity) focus on performance and concentration:
- **Vicious Mockery (Cantrip):** Iconic and provides disadvantage to enemies.
- **Suggestion (Level 2):** Powerhouse for social and combat control.
- **Greater Invisibility (Level 4):** Fantastic for support or staying safe.
- **Silvery Barbs (if allowed):** Extremely powerful for controlling rolls.

## Recommended Feats (Future Levels)
- **Resilient (Constitution):** **Highly Recommended.** Helps you maintain Concentration on your *Creation Concerto*.
- **Metamagic Adept:** Gives you options like *Subtle Spell* (great for a sneaky Bard).
- **War Caster:** If you find yourself close to combat, this helps with Concentration and casting with weapons.

## Strategy: The "Grave-Singer" Playstyle
- **Action Economy:** Use your Action for spells like *Vicious Mockery* or *Bane*, and your Bonus Action for *Bardic Inspiration* or starting your *Creation Concerto*.
- **Concentration Management:** You can only have one concentration effect (until Level 14). Choose between a powerhouse spell (like *Hold Person*) or your *Creation Concerto* (Nocturne/Requiem/Waltz) based on what the party needs most.
