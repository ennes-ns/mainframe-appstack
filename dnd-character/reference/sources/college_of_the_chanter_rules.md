# College of Virtuosity (College of the Chanter)

*Source: Pointy Hat Bard Subclass*

## Level 3: Creation Concerto
As a **Bonus Action**, expend a use of **Bardic Inspiration** to start a Creation Concerto in a **10-foot Emanation**. Choose one:

- **Nocturne:** Gain Resistance to one damage type of your choice. Up to [CHA mod] creatures in area gain **+1 AC**.
- **Requiem:** When dealing damage with a spell (not cantrip), add a Bardic Inspiration die to damage. Up to [CHA mod] creatures in area have **Advantage on Death Saving Throws**.
- **Waltz:** Double Proficiency bonus on **Dexterity saving throws**. Up to [CHA mod] creatures in area gain **+5 ft Speed**.

**Duration:** 1 minute. Requires **Concentration**.
**Change Option:** As a Bonus Action, spend another Bardic Inspiration die.

## Level 3: Magical Musician
- **Advantage** on Charisma (Performance) checks involving singing or playing an instrument.
- Can use a musical instrument focus in place of **Somatic** and **Material** components (no-cost).
